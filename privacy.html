Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and protect your privacy on this website.

Last updated: April 12, 2026

At jumanapt.ca, your privacy matters. This page explains what information we collect when you visit our website, what we do not collect, and how your data is handled.

Website Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us improve our content and ensure the site works well for everyone.

What Cloudflare Web Analytics Collects

  • Page views - Which pages are visited and how often
  • Referrer information - How you found our site (e.g., search engine, direct visit, social media)
  • Country-level location - Your general geographic region based on your connection, not your precise location
  • Browser and device type - What browser and operating system you are using (e.g., Chrome on Windows, Safari on iPhone)
  • Page load performance - How quickly pages load for visitors

What Cloudflare Web Analytics Does NOT Collect

  • No cookies - Cloudflare Web Analytics is completely cookie-free. Nothing is stored on your device.
  • No personal information - Your name, email address, phone number, or any identifying details are never collected through analytics.
  • No IP address tracking - Your IP address is not stored or used to identify you.
  • No cross-site tracking - We do not track your activity across other websites.
  • No fingerprinting - We do not use browser fingerprinting or any technique to identify individual visitors.
  • No data sold to third parties - Analytics data is never sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Cloudflare Web Analytics was specifically designed to provide website usage insights without compromising visitor privacy. You can read more about their approach in the Cloudflare Web Analytics documentation.

Affiliate Links

Some of our blog posts contain links to products on Amazon.ca through the Amazon Associates program. If you click one of these links and make a purchase, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

These links are clearly marked with a disclosure at the bottom of each blog post that contains them. You are always free to purchase similar products from any supplier of your choice.

When you click an affiliate link, Amazon may use their own cookies and tracking on their website. This is governed by Amazon's Privacy Notice, not ours.

Booking System

When you click "Book Now" on our site, you are taken to Jane App, a third-party booking platform. Any information you provide during the booking process (name, email, phone number, health information) is handled by Jane App under their own privacy policy. We do not store booking information on this website.

Contact Information

If you contact us by phone or email, we will only use your information to respond to your inquiry. We do not add contact information to marketing lists or share it with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to how we collect or handle data, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at info@jumanapt.ca.
